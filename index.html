The honest bit.

A $100 website sounds too cheap to be real. Here's the whole deal, out loud, so you never have to go looking for the fine print.

Why only $100 to start?

Building your site costs us real design time, and $100 keeps us honest and keeps the tyre-kickers out. It's a one-off buy-in, not a deposit — it covers the work either way. If you love the site, you stay at $75 a month. That's the whole model.

Who owns what?

You own your domain name and your content, your logo, photos and words about your business, always. We own the site build and hosting while you're with us. It's a rental with an option to buy.

What if I want to leave?

The monthly has no lock-in — cancel any month, and your domain and content leave with you. The $100 start fee and any add-ons aren't refundable, since the work's already done. Want the whole site's files? $1,500 flat and it's yours forever.

What's this back end I keep hearing about?

Every SNACK site comes with its own login where you see your visitor numbers, your enquiries, your site status and your requests. It's included in your monthly, not an extra.

Will Secret Sauce get me to number one on Google?

Honest answer: nobody can promise that, and you should run from anyone who does. What Secret Sauce does is the consistent work that search engines reward: regular fresh content, a healthy fast site, and tracking so you can see your progress for yourself.

What snack isn't

Online stores, booking systems and member portals are a bigger meal than a snack. If that's you, we'll happily introduce you to our full-course team. No upsell ambush, promise.
