2. The build — $100 to start.

4. Your free quarterly update.

Every quarter you're with us, you get one update to your site, free. Here's exactly what that means, so neither of us ever has to argue about it:

One update covers one of these:

  • New photos — swapping or adding images across the site (you supply them).
  • Text changes to existing pages — refreshing your About section, updating services, prices, hours, team members, that sort of thing.
  • One new page — where you provide the content, or you approve content our AI drafts from what you tell us. One page per update.

The rules, kept simple:

  • One per quarter (Jan–Mar, Apr–Jun, Jul–Sep, Oct–Dec). Updates don't roll over or stack — use it or it resets. That keeps the price at $75.
  • Send everything for the update in one go, through your back end or by email. One update = one bundled request, not a drip-feed over three months.
  • Each update includes one round of revisions after we show you the result.
  • We'll have it live within 5 business days of receiving everything we need.
  • Content is yours to supply. If you'd rather we draft it with AI from your notes, you review and approve it before it goes live — once approved, it's treated as your content.

What it doesn't cover:

  • Redesigns, layout overhauls or "can we make it feel different" jobs.
  • New functionality — stores, bookings, member areas, integrations. That's a bigger meal than a snack.
  • Logo or brand changes (we can quote those separately).
  • Anything beyond a reasonable volume — if a single "update" is really five updates in a trench coat, we'll say so and quote the difference fairly before doing anything.

Need a change that doesn't fit the freebie, or need it sooner than next quarter? Just ask — small jobs are quoted flat and in advance, and nothing is ever done without your OK.
